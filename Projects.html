TITLE:Displaying Arrows And Emojis in 8*8 LED Matrix



8*8 LED MATRIX
The objective of displaying arrows and emojis in an 8*8 LED matrix is to create a simple and visually effective way to convey directional information or emotions in a compact space. Arrows cab be used to indicate direction or movement , such as in a game or a navigation system. Emojis on the other hand can convey emotions or reactions, such as in a chat or social media platform. Adding arrows and emojis can make the display more interactive and entertaining, which can help keep users more interested.
